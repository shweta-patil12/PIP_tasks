    FAQ

    What shoes to wear with kurta?

    Kurta can be styled with both formal and casual footwear.

  • For Men - For a traditional occasion, you can wear sandals, loafers, and leather sandals to complete your look. For casual attire, you can pair your kurtas with jeans or trousers and then wear casual shoes, flip flops, sports sandals, or slip-ons to go with it.
  • For Women - For a traditional look, you can wear sandals, juttis, heels, and kolhapuri chappals with kurtas. For a more casual look, you can wear slip-ons, one-toe flats, or casual shoes.
  • For Kids - Kids can sport smart sandals for casual events and loafers for a more traditional look.

    • What types of shoes are for a party?

    For a party, you should be wearing shoes that are comfortable as well as matching your outfit so they can complement your overall look. 

  • For Men - If you are wearing formal attire, then formal shoes and loafers will go the best with it. If you are wearing a casual outfit, then slip-ons, sandals, or casual shoes will be a good choice for footwear. 
  • For Women - Women can wear heels, embellished flats to a party depending upon the outfit. For a more quirky look, women can also experiment with funky and colourful sneakers.
  • For Kids - Sneakers, sports shoes, slip-ons, ballerinas are the best to wear for parties.

    • Which are the top 5 shoe types used most?

  • For Men - Formal shoes, sneakers, sports shoes, loafers, and slip-ons are the most used.
  • For Women - Heels, flats, ballerinas, sneakers, and stilettos are some of the most used shoes.
  • For Kids - Sneakers, slip-ons, and ballerinas are the shoe types used mostly.
